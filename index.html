I have learnt html semantic and semantic css such as "nav", "header" , "article" , "section" , "aside".

I have learnt how to center text as well!

In HTML and css, nav is used to create a navigation menu.

header is used for the container of introductory content or a set of navigational links

article tag is used to group together content that makes sense on its own

section tag is used to group together thematically related content

aside tag is used to group together content that is related to the main content but not essential to it.
